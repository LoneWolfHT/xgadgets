This mod is good for any PvP world!
It adds:
 -Fire Bottle - Ignites fire!
 -Explosive Bottle - Makes and explosion!
 -Net Bottle - Makes net traps!
 -Smoke Bottle - Creates a pillar of smoke!
 -Net Trap - Slows enemies down.
 -Phantom Stone - A block that looks like stone, but you can go thraugh it!
 Enjoy!!!
